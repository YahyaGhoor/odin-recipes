Tomato Soup

Description

This is the creamiest tomato soup I have ever had.

Prep:15 mins ------- Cook:1 hr 5 mins

Total:1 hr 20 mins ------- Servings:2

Ingredients

  • 1 (29 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed chicken broth, undiluted
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tablespoon chopped onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda

Steps

  1. In a large stock pot, combine tomatoes, chicken broth, butter, sugar, onion, and baking soda.
  2. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 1 hour.
  4. Stir in cream.
  5. Cook until heated through and serve.
